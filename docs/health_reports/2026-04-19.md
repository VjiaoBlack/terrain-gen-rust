**Confirmed persistent drift (unresolved from prior checks):**

2. **VillagerMemory Phase 2 never started (Pillar 2):** `VillagerMemory` struct (DangerZone, ThreatReport) is written in systems.rs but never read in ai.rs for decisions. AI still queries global stockpile counts (~15 occurrences in ai.rs). Design intent: per-villager local knowledge drives decisions; global state should be inaccessible to agents.

3. **0 events across 9 consecutive days:** No drought, migration, harvest, or wolf surge events in any evaluation seed. Known issue: Drought requires `grain >= pop*5` (unreachable at tick 6000); ROAD_TRAFFIC_THRESHOLD=300.0 never reached by small populations.

5. **Desert biome dominance (Pillar 1):** Seeds 42 and 137 have Desert at 32% and 49% respectively; Forest < 1% in all seeds. Mountains absent from all evaluation seeds. Pillar 1 requires geography to differ fundamentally between maps, not just palette.
