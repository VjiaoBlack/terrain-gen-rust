## Design Drift

**NEW CRITICAL: Drought/harvest event modifiers silently discarded (`game/mod.rs:2135-2156`)**

`tick_config` is cloned from `sim_config` (line 2135), then drought reduces `tick_config.rain_rate *= 0.4` and harvest increases it by 1.5x (lines 2141-2148), but `step_water_cycle` is called with `(should_rain: bool, veg_growth_mult: f64)` at line 2156 — `tick_config` is never passed and goes out of scope. The simulation runs with the original unmodified `sim_config`. Even if drought events fired, they would have **zero effect** on rainfall or moisture. This contradicts the design goal (game_design.md Pillar 2): "A drought shouldn't `farm_yield *= 0.5`. It should reduce water levels → reduce soil moisture → slow crop growth." The chain exists in code but is broken at the critical handoff.

This also renders the `mods.rain_mult` evaporation modifier (line 2137) dead code.
