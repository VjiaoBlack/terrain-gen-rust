## Design Drift

### No events ever fire (all 3 seeds, tick 12000)
All diagnostic runs show `"events":[]`. Analysis:
- **Wolf spawn**: checked every 3000 ticks at Spring multiplier 0.5× → ~5.8% chance per check → ~16% cumulative over 3 Spring checks. Possible to see zero wolves, but 84% probability each run.
- **Drought**: requires `grain >= villagers*5` in Summer. At tick 12000 (Summer just started): seed 42 has grain=38 needs 40, seed 137 has grain=40 needs 60, seed 777 has grain=28 needs 40. All fail the buffer check — drought structurally cannot fire at 12K ticks.
- **Implication**: Drought threshold (`grain >= pop*5`) is too high relative to early-game production rates. The "Emergent Complexity from Simple Agents" pillar requires events to fire regularly.
- **Diagnostic gap**: `threat_score` is not exposed in the diagnostics JSON — makes event readiness invisible.

### Villager omniscience (Pillar 2 gap)
Design docs describe per-villager memory, local awareness, stockpile bulletin board — none implemented. Design docs exist in `docs/design/pillar2_emergence/` but are unimplemented. Known and tracked.

### All games still Year 1 at tick 12000
No game has progressed to Year 2. The Explore→Expand→Exploit→Endure arc is not observable at this timescale. Seasons cycle correctly within Year 1 (Spring → Summer confirmed), but inter-year changes/escalation have not been tested.

### Desert dominance
Desert consistently 40-54% across all seeds. The design goal "terrain asymmetry between maps" is partially met (seed 137 differs with 30% water vs 17% in seed 42), but the Desert biome's dominance suggests the biome tuning may need revisiting.
