**New finding (Architecture):** `--screenshot` mode sets `half_speed_base=true` (`main.rs:206`). This means `--ticks 12000` runs 12000 step() calls but advances `game.tick` by only ~6000 (every other step is skipped). All historical evaluations have been at `game.tick=6000`, not 12000 — the health check spec's "12000 ticks" is misleading. Baselines use `"tick": 12000` to mean "step count", not game tick. Added harness Check #20 to guard this.
